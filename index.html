Repository Purpose

CLIP-seq can globally report all the binding positions of RNA binding proteins, like PTB, FOX, etc. The key is to identify the sites where proteins and RNA are bound with high affinity and strength, a.k.a peak finding. Lots of Chip-seq tools had been utilized to find peak of CLIP-seq as both Chip-seq and CLIP-seq scan protein-nucleic interaction sites.
